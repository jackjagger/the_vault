A type of business that creates products from raw and unrefined materials. Typical [[transactions]] for these types of businesses include:

- Raw material costs and inventory evalutaion
- Direct labour expenses
- Overhead including building and business-specific machine operation expenses
- Asset depreciation

An example of a manufacturing business would be an oil refinery company who takes raw crude oil and uses specialised machinery to turn into useable oil for transportation, which can be sold to [[retail business]] outlets such as petrol stations.

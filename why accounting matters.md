Accounting is the act of collating, explaining and synthesising financial information such that important information relating to a businesses or individuals goals are presented intentionaly to them and the right decisions can be made based on the practices taken.

All individuals in the professional world require that their yearly tax return is completed with complete transparency and honesty; this can be a difficult endeavour if they have no clue of basic accounting practices that keep evidence of their spending and profit habits in their day-to-day and business lives.
